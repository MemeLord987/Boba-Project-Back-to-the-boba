Indie Game's Are Goated and Here Are Some You Should Play :)

Hello I'm Alexander Cua back with another website. In this website I talk about some of the Indie Game that I have played and would recomend to people if they want a new interesting game to play.

Five Nights at Freddy's

Five Nights At Freddy's is a horror game where you have to survive against animatronics using doors to close them of and cameras to help survive the night. For me Five Nights at Freddy's is a simple horror game great for replaying and horror is great.

Hollow Knight

Hollow Knight is a Metroidvania style game where you play as the knight exporing the place of Hallownest where you encounter other bugs, enemies and bosses to fight. This game is a top favorite for me because of it's great art work and it's great immersive environment. This game is perfect for anyone looking a game to explore around.

Undertale

Undertale is a game where you play as a fallen child that finds themselves in the monster world where they travel through all the unique places where the playes actions affect the story. For me Undertales story was one of its strongest factors when playing the game and I loved how the action us as the player do affect which ending we get.

Celeste

Celeste is a platformer where you play as a girl named Madeline who attempts to climb Celeste Mountain to prove herself and over come her fears. On her journey up she encounters hard obsticals, friendly faces and places she would never expect to see on a mountain. Celeste is one of the harder game here on this list but that difficulty is what make it so rewarding to finaly complete a chapter. It's trial and error sort of gameplay is was got to to keep trying new ways to find how to beat levels and keep progressing.

Ultrakill

Ultrakill is a First Person Shoot game where you play as a robot named V1 where you must fight through layers of hell. This is the game I've played the least so far but is fast and colorful gameplay keeps me immersed and going through levels.

Cuphead(AKA Da Head)

Cuphead is a run and gun game with an old 1960's art style about two Brothers who gambled their Souls away to the Devil. In order to save themsevels from the devil they are tasked to collest the soul contracts from other of the devils victims. For me Cuphead is my top 1 favorite Indie game of all time. I great art style, difficult gameplay, mechanics and replayabilty allow me to come back and play for hours. All of the interesting charms and weapons help make fighting bosses more easy or more challenging for what every challenge you may want to do. Another great thing about this game is that you can play with a second player and I have very fond memories of me and my brother playing together and beating bosses.
